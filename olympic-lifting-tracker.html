Coefficients
These multipliers estimate your implied 1RM on the competition lift when you log a variation. Each modifier makes the lift harder or easier relative to the floor squat standard. Position coefficients reflect bar height and pull length. Receiving coefficients reflect catch depth and demand. Technique coefficients reflect constraints like pauses. They stack multiplicatively. Defaults are population averages from Oly coaching literature -- your own logged data overrides them automatically once you have direct variation matches.
